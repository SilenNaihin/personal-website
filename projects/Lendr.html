Lendr

About

Lendr was started due to predatory payday loans that immigrants and others with low credit score must resort to. We set out to get to the root of the problem and provide an alternative choice.

Introduction

Having a low credit score puts you into a vicous cycle. To be able to take out a loan or build credit, you have to have credit. But if you don't have credit in the first place, how are you supposed to build credit?

  • Immigrants and people with low credit scores are forced to take out predatory payday loans
  • These are loans that have insane interest rates as shown in the picture to the right
  • Banks are using outdated systems of measuring credit scores

Idea

That's why we created Lendr. The solution is to use a reinforcement learning algorithm to create a more accurate credit score based on over 200 factors. This includes factors that banks don't have such as social media presence (which may be correlated to being more trustworthy), and more.

  • The app is also a peer to peer loaning marketplace, which bypasses the need for a bank
  • This leads to more accurate credit scores
  • Making affordable loans accessible to all!

More

This project was made for the Elevate Tech Jam Hackathon. My main roles were front end coding for the website and working on the slide deck. Started with Luke Piette, Talha Atta, and Thayallen Srinathan.
